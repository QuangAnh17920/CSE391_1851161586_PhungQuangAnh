    About

    Couch Creative is a full service communication agency, where intelligence meets imagination. We have been providing high-level strategic communications advice and solutions to our valued clients since 2001. Couch Creative is a specialist at delivering integrated campaigns for both State and Federal governments, as well as private companies, industry and community organisations.

As a solution-focused agency, we research and implement ‘best case’ solutions for specific clients, challenges, briefs and projects. Whether custom, proprietary or a mixture of both, our solutions perform well because they are informed by incisive analysis, integrated expert knowledge and experience in both traditional and digital communication channels.
